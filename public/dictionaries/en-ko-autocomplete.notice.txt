VocaLoop English-Korean autocomplete dictionary

This compact autocomplete index was generated from:
https://github.com/marcinchs/krdict-reader

The upstream krdict-reader project describes its data as an English-Korean
dictionary mainly based on the National Institute of Korean Language's
Korean-English Learners' Dictionary, with additional entries from Extended Open
Multilingual Wordnet.
